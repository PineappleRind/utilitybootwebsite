Frequently Asked Questions

What can the bot do?

Utility Bot has many useful commands. You can kick, ban, or mute members of your server. There are also some fun commands such as roast or slap.

Which prefix does UtilityBot use?

The default prefix is u!. It can be changed easily. Example: u!mute

How do I know what commands there are?

All of the commands are listed on our command list.
